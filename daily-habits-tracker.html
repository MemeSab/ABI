Welcome to Your Tracker

"One small action today is better than waiting for the perfect moment."

This tracker is designed to help you build awareness around your daily habits without judgment. Building sustainable health isn't about checking every box perfectly—it's about the simple, repeating practices that honor your body and restore your mind.

Use this booklet to follow your daily intentions, reflect on your small wins, and map out your week. Remember, if you miss a habit or have an off day, show yourself compassion. Every day is a fresh opportunity to make the next right choice.

Abigail Stocks

How to Use This Booklet

  • Print It Out: Keep this tracker on your bedside table or workspace where you can see it daily.
  • Set Your Morning Mindset: Start your day by checking off morning intentions and defining one simple focus.
  • Daily Reflection: Take 3 minutes before bed to review your wins, gratitude, and rate your overall energy.
  • Weekly Check-in: At the end of the week, complete the check-in to reflect on your progress and focus areas.
