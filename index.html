Meet Abigail

About Abigail Stocks

Abigail Stocks is a passionate Healthy Habits Coach dedicated to helping women build realistic, sustainable health and wellbeing practices that fit into everyday life.

Her journey began at the age of three through dance and performance, leading her to complete a Performing Arts Diploma and a Degree in Dance and Movement. Movement has always been more than exercise to Abigail; it has been a lifelong expression of self-care, resilience, and respect for the body.

As a wife of 28 years, mother of six, and proud grandmother of two, Abigail understands the challenge of prioritising personal wellbeing whilst caring for others. Her coaching is compassionate, practical, and designed for real life.

The Philosophy

Consistency Over Perfection

Moving away from all-or-nothing thinking to build lasting health habits that fit into your genuine daily routine.

Abigail’s coaching philosophy is centred around helping women move away from all-or-nothing thinking. Through her Consistent Health Habit Method, she supports clients in creating small, meaningful habits that lead to lasting change without guilt, pressure, or unrealistic expectations.

01

Small, Manageable Steps

Starting with tiny changes that require minimal willpower, making them easy to integrate and maintain even on busy days.

02

Self-Compassion First

Replacing critical inner dialogues and guilt with a supportive coaching atmosphere that nurtures lasting behavior shifts.

03

Sustainable Rhythms

Tailoring practices to respect the changing seasons of a woman's life, creating habits that adapt rather than break.
